<h1>Starting Mai-Tai Mode</h1>
<p className="lead">Tell your agent to connect and start chatting.</p>

<h2>How to Start</h2>
<p>Simply tell your AI agent:</p>
<pre><code>"start mai-tai mode"</code></pre>

<h2>What Happens</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Agent connects to your workspace</li>
  <li>Agent waits for messages from you</li>
  <li>You send messages from Mai-Tai (web or mobile)</li>
  <li>Agent responds in real-time</li>
</ol>

<h2>Stopping</h2>
<p>Just close the agent, or tell it to stop. The workspace stays available for next time.</p>
